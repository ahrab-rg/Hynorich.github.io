About HynoRich

HynoRich is an Afrobeat/Afro pop singer whose journey to stardom is a testament to resilience and dedication. Hailing from Akwatia in the Eastern Region of Ghana, Hyno grew up with a passion for music that led him from his early days in Ada to the bustling streets of Accra. Detecting his singing talent early, he left home to chase his dreams, recording his first tracks at Clinchy Records in Madina before finding his creative home at Flavor Records in Adenta. There, at 75% of his full potential, HynoRich honed his craft, blending infectious African rhythms, global pop sounds, and soulful vocals into music that resonates deeply.

His debut single, “Love,” was inspired by a real-life love story—a pretty girl he fell for in Tema back in the day. Hyno’s music is about feeling happy in this messy world, capturing the struggles and triumphs of his journey. Offstage, Hyno is a cool dude everyone loves being around, and as a member of the Rich Gang, he’s ready to make his mark on the global stage. The future is bright for HynoRich, and his music is here to connect people with the heartbeat of African culture.
